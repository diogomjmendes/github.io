About

Connecting rigorous research with real-world healthcare

I am an Assistant Professor at the Faculty of Pharmacy, University of Coimbra, and CEO and co-founder of Clevidence. My work spans pharmacoepidemiology, pharmacoeconomics, health technology assessment (HTA), and health economics and outcomes research (HEOR).

I focus on generating and synthesising robust evidence from clinical studies and real-world data. The aim is to support decisions that balance patient access to therapeutic innovation with the sustainability of healthcare systems.

Areas of focus

From evidence generation to policy and access

01

Health Technology Assessment

Relative effectiveness, added therapeutic value, reimbursement and evidence requirements for healthcare decision-making.

02

HEOR & Pharmacoeconomics

Economic evaluation, outcomes research, value assessment and sustainable access to medicines and health technologies.

03

Pharmacoepidemiology & RWE

Use of real-world data to understand the safety, effectiveness and utilisation of medicines in clinical practice.

04

Evidence Synthesis

Systematic reviews, meta-analysis and network meta-analysis to translate complex evidence into actionable insight.
